History of Dholl Puri Cuisine

Founded in 2021 by Rashmi Govind, Dholl Puri Cuisine reflects this diversity and cultural influences of Mauritius.

At Dholl Puri Cuisine's, we cook local food such as dholl puris, rotis, ti puris along with different filings.

Savoury Rotis

The dough of rotis is made by hand using very hot water which is kneaded for 30 to 40 mins in order to have a consistent and soft dough.

After, it is thinly rolled to have a round shape.

Customers are satisfied as we use different types of flour to increase its taste. In the making of the dough, very little oil is used in order to decrease choresterol issues.

Puris

They are deep fried Indian breads made of self-raising flour and white flour that are golden and crispy.

No oil is used in the making of puris at Dholl Puri Cuisine.

Delicious Dholl Puri

We make the dholl puri as thin as paper and soft. You can be guaranteed a soft, melt-in-your-mouth dholl puri with different fillings slopped in.
